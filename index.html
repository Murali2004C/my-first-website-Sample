About Institution

SIST strives to be a world-class university, committed to creating and disseminating knowledge, fostering innovation, and empowering students to make a positive impact on society. SRMIST, initially known as SRM Engineering College, gained deemed university status in 2002. It is a well-established institution with a strong reputation in India. History & Growth Of SIST GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS reflects the inspiring and visionary Founder – Chancellor Leadership, the committed Pro-Chancellors of Administration and Academics, Vice President, Vice Chancellor, Pro-VCs, Registrars, and the 3000+ Faculty and Staff Members and our vibrant large group of SIST Alumni and Students.At SRMIST, we focus on career-oriented growth as well as versatility and diverse first-career options. There has been a consistent growth in our placements record. Today, graduating does not mean getting a degree certificate alone but also equipping one with in-depth knowledge of the subject. It has also become mandatory that the student instils both technical and soft skills within him in order to reach heights. Learning is an enjoyable experience here, with experienced faculty, inspiring libraries and stimulating discussion rooms, large playgrounds and state-of-the-art labs. All in all about 42 blocks sprawling across 250 acres of greenery filled campus with not one, but two lakes and wonderful walkways.

Holistic Learning Experience @ SIST


SIST Directorate of Admissions manages admissions for students. SRM is among India’s earliest large multidisciplinary higher educational institutions that now caters 50000+ students across 6 campuses. SIST Directorate of Admissions manages admissions for students from across India as well as for International students from all around the world.
SIST is often seen as a Mini-India. SRM is an ideal destination for those aspiring to pursue under-graduate, post-graduate, PhD or advanced studies. The promise of an enriching student life experience in a vastly multi-cultural, green and high ranked institution are but few reasons you must consider SIST for your higher education.
